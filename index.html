Box Star Unfolding

Dani Demas and Yu Xuan Hong

This visualization shows the star unfolding for a rectangular prism. When this is folded into a box, the red points will meet at the the source point of the unfolding. The purple lines are the edges of the Voronoi diagram of the red points. These edges should also be the cut locus of the source point.

Click and drag any red point to change the location of the source point (which is restricted to one of the width-by-height faces)
